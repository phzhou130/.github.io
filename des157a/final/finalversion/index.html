WHO GETS THE TREATS

Huhu is a shiba inu and Hopi is a cat.
They both live in the ZHOU's family.
Help them to get the treats they wanted!
RULE:
Two players are needed for this game.
Player1: Press the A(move left) and D(move right) key to control the dog HUHU's bowl.
Player2: Press the left and right key to control the cat Hopi's bowl.
Catch the treats with the bowls.
The player who reach 15 points first will win the game.
